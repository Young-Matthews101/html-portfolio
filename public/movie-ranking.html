The Best Movies According to Marlon

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

The Wood

The Wood is a coming of age film that watches 3 young men that grew up with each other in Inglewood. The movie explores friendship, family and most of all love

Love & Basketball

Love & Basketball is a film about two young sportstars finding the balance between love and the game they both love dearly basketball. Basketball takes them both on a journey of self-discovery with ups and downs

The Best Man

The Best man is a film starring the usual suspects of Morris Chestnut, Taye Diggs, Sanaa Lathan and Terrance Howard just to name a few. Lance (Morris Chestnut) Finds himself getting married and with Harper (Taye Diggs) back in town promoting his new book, a lot of hidden truths come to the surface during the lead up to the wedding
